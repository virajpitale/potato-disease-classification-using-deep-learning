Potato Leaf Disease Classifier

Upload an image of a potato leaf to determine if it is healthy or affected.

Disease Classification

Healthy: The potato leaf is in good condition, showing no signs of disease. Proper growth and development without any visible abnormalities.

Early Blight: Characterized by brown spots with yellow halos on the leaves. This disease can cause premature leaf drop and reduce crop yield if not managed.

Late Blight: Known for dark, water-soaked lesions on the leaves, which may turn into a grayish mold. This serious disease can cause significant damage to the potato crop if not controlled.
